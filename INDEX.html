What is a game genre?

A game genre is a category that groups games based on how they are played and what they are about. It helps people understand the type of experience a game offers.



What is a game

A video game is a type of game that is played using an electronic device, such as a computer, console, smartphone, or tablet. It involves interacting with images on a screen, usually by using a controller, keyboard, mouse, or touch controls.

📖 What defines a video game?

A video game is an interactive electronic experience combining visuals, sound, and rules. Players engage through controllers, keyboards, or touch — solving challenges, competing, or exploring narratives. From solo story-driven epics to massive online battles, games have evolved into the largest entertainment medium worldwide.
